Nicotine:

You can also ask yourself these questions to determine whether someone is a smoking addict:

Does this person’s breath or body smell like smoke?
Is this person opening the window no matter the weather to get rid of a smoke smell?
Does this person use a lighter?
Does this person use a match?
Does this person’s clothes or other accessories have burnt holes?
Does this person use an ashtray frequently?
Does this person have yellow or brown fingernails?
Is this person depressed often?
Does this person have less energy to do activities?
Do you notice this person having yellow teeth quickly?
Does this person lose breath quickly occasionally?
Is this person’s allowance draining quickly?
Is this person recently having poor academic performance?
Is this person watching movies or digital content that have people smoking in them?

Of course, a person needs to fulfill most of these signs to be considered a smoking addict.
