Nicotine:

Treatment:

The question now is: how can you get one to stop smoking? One can download the QuitGuide Mobile App created by the CDC to help develop skills needed to quit smoking. You can also talk to a doctor about it. Also, one can call the free hotline, 1-800-QUIT-NOW (1-800-784-8669), to get support in withdrawing from smoking. Visit the CDC website for more tips and advice to stop smoking.
